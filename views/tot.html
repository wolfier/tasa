WHAT IS TASTE OF TAIWAN?
Taste of Taiwan is a cultural event held by TASA, Taiwanese American Student Association. Join other students for a night like none other, full of fun games, shocking performances, and not to mention FREE FOOD. It's the perfect opportunity to meet and mingle with other like-minded students, all the while learning more about Taiwan.

We welcome all those not allergic to FUN!

FAQ
WHEN WILL TASTE OF TAIWAN TAKE PLACE?
Taste of Taiwan will begin on Thursday, March 31, 2016, from 7PM to 10PM.
A more detailed schedule will be released closer to the actual date of the event.
WHO CAN COME?
Anyone who is interested in eating authentic food or experiencing cultural activities. Or maybe you've got time to spare! Take a chance and see new things!
HOW MUCH DOES ATTENDING COST?
Admission is free, and includes food, drinks, and miscellaneous swag for the entire event!
I'M INTERESTED IN PERFORMING! WHO SHOULD I CONTACT?
All interested parties should contact Chris Liu, our awesome external officer, through his email, tasa.external@gmail.com.
WHAT FOOD WILL YOU PROVIDE?
Delicious asian cuisine will be catered throughout the event, as well as some home made xiaochi, or snack food, specific to regions in Taiwan! Come at home made green onion pancakes! We also have traditional soy milk and fried dough stick. However, quantity is limited so come early!
WHAT IF IT RAINS THAT DAY?
The event will still continue. Mulitple tents will be available!
